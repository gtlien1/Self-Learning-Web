lasagna

Apparently the best lasagna in the world

The instructions following will walk you through making the best lasagna in the world

Ingredients

  • Meat (we suggest Italian sausage and lean ground beef)
  • Onion and garlic (one onion and two cloves of garlic)
  • Tomato products (we suggest a can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste)
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (parsley, basil, salt, black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
